About Jafrapur

Jafrapur is a Village in Nirmal Mandal in Adilabad District of Andhra Pradesh State, India. It belongs to Telangana region . It is located 87 KM towards South from District head quarters Adilabad. 9 KM from Nirmal. 214 KM from State capital Hyderabad Jafrapur Pin code is 504105 and postal head office is Soan . Madhapur ( 2 KM ) , Kadthal ( 3 KM ) , Siddankunta ( 4 KM ) , Soan ( 4 KM ) , Koutla (k) ( 4 KM ) are the nearby Villages to Jafrapur. Jafrapur is surrounded by Laxmanchanda Mandal towards East , Dilawarpur Mandal towards west , Balkonda Mandal towards South , Mamda Mandal towards East .
Nirmal , Bhainsa , Koratla , Nizamabad are the nearby Cities to Jafrapur.
Demographics of Jafrapur
Telugu is the Local Language here. Total population of Jafrapur is 1547 .Males are 747 and Females are 800 living in 349 Houses. Total area of Jafrapur is 690 hectares.
